ABOUT ME

A lover of the outdoors and Software Engineering Student

I consider myself a lifelong student. Of life. Of parenthood. And now of Software Engineering. My discovery of the soothing nature of being outdoors has led me to an endless pursuit of wanting to be outside and away from the hustle and bustle of life. My hope is that changing my career at this stage of my life will give me more opportunity to be outdoors while giving me the skills to create and to be a contributing member to society. I have been a chef for ten years and now look forward to the less busy side of cooking while exercising a different part of my brain. I have studied Python, HTML, CSS, C#, and dabbled in some Networking courses. I look forward to learning and growing more within the Software Engineering and Web Developement program at New Engalnd Institute of Technology.
